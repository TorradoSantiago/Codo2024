# Executive Brief

## In one sentence

Demo website for a hardware and tech-support business, designed as an early commercial web showcase.

## What problem it addresses

It shows how a simple static website can be reorganized into a clearer presentation of services, products, and contact paths.

## What a non-technical reader can see

- A more structured homepage.
- A demo product and service catalog.
- Contact and client-access flows.

## What it demonstrates

- Basic frontend execution.
- Commercial presentation and visual organization.
- Ability to turn a technical deliverable into a more business-friendly asset.

## Best next step

Connect the contact flow to a real channel and add a minimal conversion or inquiry metric.
